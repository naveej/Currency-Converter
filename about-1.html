    This is a currency calculator with third-party API. There is almost all currency available, you can check the exchange rate of any currency comparing to others. The exchange rates are depending on a third-party API, that’s why it will update on global rate changes. The whole program is based on pure JavaScript, no other library or framework. This is a simple program but it is perfectly styled.
